Changing your network status

By default, your network status is set to Available to All Users. This mode enables you to;
  • be seen by other online users,
  • send & receive text messages,
  • share your files and receive shared files,
  • share your views and receive shared views.
You can set your network status to Do Not Disturb from the Collaboration menu, under Network Status item as shown in the following figure. In this mode, all features listed above will be disabled.
